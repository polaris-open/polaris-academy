# Support

> **Status: draft v0.1.** Community is support, not dependency.

Polaris Academy is **self-paced first**. The learning path is designed to work even
if you study 100% alone. Community channels help you go faster — they are a safety
net, never a prerequisite.

## Where to get help

- **Troubleshooting first.** Each level ships a troubleshooting guide. For Level 1,
  see
  [the Level 1 troubleshooting](formations/applied-ai-engineering/levels/01-ai-foundations-for-builders/troubleshooting.md).
- **Discussions / issues.** Use the repository's GitHub Discussions and Issues for
  questions, peer review and showcases. Organization-wide support channels are
  listed in [`polaris-open/.github`](https://github.com/polaris-open/.github).

## How to ask a good question

Help the community help you:

- **Context** — what you are trying to do and at which level/step.
- **Reproduction** — the exact command you ran and the full output/error.
- **What you already tried.**
- **Goal** — what "working" would look like.

> Reminder: never paste secrets, real PII, customer data or corporate data into a
> question. Synthetic data only.

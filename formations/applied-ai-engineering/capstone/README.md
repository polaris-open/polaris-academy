# Applied AI Engineering Capstone

> **Applied AI Engineering Capstone — planned.**
>
> **Status: draft v0.1.** Materials for the capstone have not been built yet.

The capstone is where you deliver the final version of the *AI Incident & Support
Assistant* and **defend it**. It does not introduce new content — it **integrates,
hardens and defends** what you built in Levels 1–3.

## Honest scope

- **This is not a certification.**
- **It does not promise a "specialist" title.** "Specialist" is a **direction of
  maturity**, not a promise. The capstone is **evidence of readiness**, not a
  credential.
- Self-certified completion is **public evidence of learning**, not an official
  Polaris credential. Any future badge names the **mode** (self / peer / cohort /
  mentored), never "specialist".
